Gorgeous art style, witty writing, interesting story & colourful characters.

If you enjoy collecting & hoarding ingredients and crafting interesting items/spells/concoctions, this game will satisfy all that.

There's no forced time constraints & it is very forgiving of errors & injuries (no real combat) so you can really take your time and enjoy exploring the world that's been created here.

Control-wise, there's flexibility with keymapping to your preference with keyboard, controller, or you could get away with just using your mouse for the entire game.

Well worth your time if you enjoy exploring, collecting, crafting, a little dark humour & uncovering the truth.

I really enjoyed this. Interesting environments & soundtrack, with very little pressure to progress quickly.
This game doesn't give too much direction, no dialogue, rather it relies on you exploring and discovering things for yourself.

Completing puzzles & unlocking skills/upgrades gradually allows you full access to the world as you eventually grow strong enough to take on the biggest of foes with ease (very satisfying).

I would say controller is a must, and even then you definitely need to be careful in some places to avoid falling to a fast death! (Though the game saves regularly so you're not really punished for dying).

If you don't like puzzlers to hold your hand too much & enjoy being shown more than told, give this one a go.

Once you accept that the game only gives a certain amount of content per day you can really just enjoy unlocking those new bits of story & relax in the beautiful atmosphere the game creates. The gorgeous art style & lovely music make it a perfect wind-down ritual for your day. You can always find extra things to do with collecting fish, shells & other items as well as decorating, though so far I've been content spending about an hour or so on the game each time. Sometimes the hidden items are tricky to see, but if you get desperate you can pay for directions.

You cannot binge this (without time travel, which doesn't interest me) - if you don't mind just playing a little each day, and would enjoy a less demanding life sim, give this gem a go.
