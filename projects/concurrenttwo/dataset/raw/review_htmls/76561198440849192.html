First Impression was of Day9 playing the game, and just a few minutes of watching I knew what I was going to buy when I got home.

Survival of the fittest and the smartest because planning ahead is a MUST in this game. The ability to stock pile food and survive or play it risky and die is a choice that will keep players on the edge of their seats. You will feel strong emotions as your town grows, empathy will rush over you when your first disaster happens and the rage that you will feel when your town is starving and the farmers die before they can harvest the food.

I give this game a 5/5 for its current state and cannot wait to see what else the developer adds. Notice how I said Develeoper? and not Devs? Or Developers, thats because the entire game was made by one person. My hats off to you sir.
