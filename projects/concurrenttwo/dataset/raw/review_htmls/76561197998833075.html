I was going to give this game a thumbs up. it was great until you get about half way through (unlocking about half the levels). At which point the game becomes absurdly difficult. Like mega difficult. It goes from being simply challenging and thought provoking to "OMG this feels practically impossible". Not fun at all at that point.

I shouldnt feel like I need to look up the 1 way to beat a level on youtube because the game is so limited in how you can do it.

Even without using any of the modifiers that make the game even harder (seriously?), its still way too challenging.

Then you have the gem upgrade system. When will developers learn that +1% is NOT fun. Small numbers that are barely noticeable simply arent fun. 1% recharge rate on something that takes 5 seconds to recharge, is a 0.1 second increase of attack speed. I dont know about you, but I cant notice the difference between 5 seconds and 4.5 seconds. By the time you have invested enough gems in it you can get it to 5% or so, which is still just not a fun number. Just too small to really feel like its being improved.

Like I said, it was fun until you hit this difficulty wall, which seems to be about half of the games levels. If you like TD games, and you can get the game super cheap to play through those, go for it. Otherwise, just avoid this game. There are more fun and good feeling TD games out there.
