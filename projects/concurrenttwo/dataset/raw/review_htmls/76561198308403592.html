Wreckfest can be fun, but the game has stability issues. Running on Win10 in 64 and 32 bit modes, and the game just stops working. This happens about 50% of the time I try to play. 6 hours in and I have had this game crash more than 10 times. I have no stability issues with any other games and all of my drivers are up to date. This is a quality issue that needs to be addressed.

Regarding the game, the performance is good, I'm able to keep 144fps. The graphics are good, but I'm not too impressed with the color customization (some bumpers cannot be painted - WHY?). The "Mustang" can only be fitted with a red roll cage. Helmets cannot be painted, is only white. The best looking wheels (rims) have goofy paint jobs that have green paint on the rim and the wheel and that cannot be removed. It just seems like whoever is in charge of customization did a half-assed job. The art department should do better.

The soundtrack isn't very good, but that can be silenced. The difficulty is hard but whatever, I don't mind a challenge. I will say that upgrading your car is a waste of credits, because all of the opponent cars will also be upgraded to be a challenge, so there's no reason to spend the money on upgrades that make you go faster. Traction and braking both suck in this game and there's no way to improve those, so don't bother.

Actually, my biggest gripe is that the game features a car that is essentially Mad Max's Ford Falcon, but Bugbear didn't capitalize on this by including the famous front splitter from that car. What a waste of potential.

So, for some tips. Remember that any game mode that is a race should be used with the lightest, smallest car available. Yep, those stupid looking Eurotrash cars are the ones you need to use for racing, not anything large that actually looks cool. Tiny cars handle and stop faster, which is all that wins races. For smashing modes, use the big cars. And for all of the missions that require you to destroy XX enemies with a bus, just prepare to get frustrated and keep repeating those races until you get lucky enough to win. Good luck with that.....

Not sure why anyone wouldn't be happy with this game, it's extremely addictive. Yeah, it may considered P2W but technically you could earn all weapons in the game by just grinding, even though that would suck. I have spent a little in the game, but it's free to play, so that's just the way things are in these games.

Regading the game itself, it's a mash-up of Mad Max and Twisted Metal, with complete customization and a bit of sci-fi added for variety. The matches are quick, usually done within 5 minutes. The maps are very well made, but there are not a lot of them. I hope thay add more, since I can see people saying they're tired of the same battlezones.

There are a few factions that focus on different playtypes. One is heavy armor but lower speed, another faction focuses on speed, etc.

Very fun game and the graphics are great. Plus they're always releasing new content.

I definitely recommend this game to people looking for something like World of Tanks but faster and with more customization.
