Short but good! Not sure why other reviews are saying the ending was bad or that it was confusing- I got a bad ending the first time and it still made sense to me. There's multiple endings, and if you go back and get different ones it starts to make more and more sense. Took me about 3 hours to finish the first time. Spoiler but not involving the plot at all After you finish the first time you can start from the beginning of any chapter, which is really nice to get all of the endings faster. None of the extra achievements are too difficult to figure out on your own, but some of the endings are a bit difficult to find. There are hints in the dialogue.

Overall I would definitely recommend this game. I got it on sale for only $3. Definitely worth it to pass the time for 3-4 hours

Super trippy and fun game. Bought this on an impulse and absolutely don't regret it. Took me about 4 hours to get through the game playing casually and enjoying finding little secrets along the way.

At 9.4 hours right now trying to 100% the game. Some of the secrets are easier to find, and others I don't think I would've ever found without a guide. Thankfully I found a guide that gives hints, so I still get to do my own discovering but I know what room it is in.

I love playing this game. I wish there was a bit more comedy in the writing, and I wish the story was just a tad longer. I did somehow get out of bounds exactly one time, and I was REALLY messing around. I would definitely recommend this game, worth the price for sure. Would love to play a sequel some day
