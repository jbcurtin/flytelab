Another great boomer shooter. Short but sweet. If you like weird and amazing looking level design, you'll love this. Check out the trailer for more.

The different chapters/worlds has different environments and every one of them is a spectacle, that represents the different factions that control them very well. Each one has different enemies so each world feels very unique and different, so you definitely won't get bored. Most of the fun for me came from the weird and amazing looking levels they made. The gameplay is pretty great too, average boomer shooter things. You have, I don't know, 7 weapons or so, plus a BFG, and each weapon has a supercharged mode, which you can activate if you fill up a meter by picking up gems from killed enemies. There is a bit of platforming and the worlds are not just linear straightforward stuff, again, it lives up to older shooters with keys, some backtracking and figuring out the way forward in a world with weird architecture and...well everything.

Oh yeah, and there's a weapon, which is pretty much acts as a rocket launcher in terms for gameplay, but in the lore, it's actually summons ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ whole planets and suns with each shot. So you can hurl miniaturized planets and suns toward enemies.
